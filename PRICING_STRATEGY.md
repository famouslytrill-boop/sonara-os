# Pricing Strategy

SONARA is priced for low-budget founders, creators, and small service businesses while remaining sustainable after payment processing fees.

## Ladder

- Free: $0 for readiness checklist and product path selection.
- Starter: $7/month for one workspace, basic offer, intake, checklist tools, and limited records.
- Core: $19/month as best value for one studio, customer records, offer records, launch readiness, and support queue.
- Pro: $39/month for all three studios, deeper records, campaign planning, advanced readiness, and priority support queue.
- Business Builder Setup: $99 one-time guided setup package.

## Future owner-review tiers

- Agency/Team: $79/month.
- Done-With-You Launch Setup: $299-$499 one-time.

Avoid $1-$3 paid tiers because payment processing fees consume too much margin.
