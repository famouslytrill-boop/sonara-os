# Open Source License Policy

SONARA uses open-source and public research responsibly. External projects are research candidates until license, security, safety, commercial-use, maintenance, and product-fit review is complete.

## License Gates

- Allowed: MIT, BSD, Apache-2.0, and other permissive licenses after attribution and dependency review.
- Review Required: MPL, LGPL, unclear dual licenses, generated-code licenses, model licenses, complex dependency chains, and projects with unclear commercial-use terms.
- Restricted: GPL and AGPL code for proprietary app packages unless legal review approves a compliant use mode.
- Blocked: unknown-license assets, private assets, copyrighted assets without rights, and non-commercial model weights in paid or commercial product features.

## Product Rules

- A listed project is not automatically bundled, endorsed, installed, copied, self-hosted, or exposed as an integration.
- Third-party provider costs may apply when a user enables optional services such as Stripe, Supabase, OpenRouter, email, SMS, storage, or model providers.
- Premium setup services, hosted infrastructure, higher limits, automation, collaboration, support, business-grade workflows, and admin controls may be paid.
- Basic public browsing must not require a payment setup or third-party API key.

## Required Human Review

Owner, legal, and security review is required before restricted projects, unclear licenses, risky automation, scraping/crawling tools, unofficial messaging automation, or model weights move beyond research/reference status.

This document is a review-ready policy draft and is not legal advice.
