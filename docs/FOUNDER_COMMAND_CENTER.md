# Founder Command Center

The Founder Command Center is a launch-readiness and operating-discipline dashboard.

It should show:

- launch readiness score
- product readiness score
- company readiness score
- marketing readiness score
- profit readiness score
- monetization readiness score
- store readiness score
- PWA readiness score
- Stripe readiness
- Supabase readiness
- Vercel readiness
- Google Play readiness
- safe autonomy readiness
- moat strength
- open blockers
- next 5 actions

Before production control surfaces are added, this route must be protected by auth, role checks, and audit logging.
