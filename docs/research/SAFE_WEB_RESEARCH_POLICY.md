# Safe Web Research Policy

SONARA Research Lab may summarize public or user-authorized sources only when the source is permitted, rate limited, and documented.

## Rules

- Ask the user to confirm ownership or permission for user-provided URLs.
- Respect robots.txt and provider terms.
- Prefer official APIs, public datasets, and customer-provided exports.
- Store source notes and summaries only after database/RLS review.
- Do not collect private, sensitive, credentialed, paywalled, or platform-prohibited content.

## Current Status

Live crawling is disabled. `/research-lab/crawling` is a policy scaffold and intake placeholder only.
