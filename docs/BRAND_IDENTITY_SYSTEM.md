# Brand Identity System

This is a working brand identity system, not trademark clearance.

## Strategy

The ecosystem uses adaptive identity, geometric simplicity, digital-first scalability, app-icon readiness, high contrast, dark-mode compatibility, and color-coded product hierarchy.

## Brand Hierarchy

- Parent Company: SONARA Industries
- Creator / Music Technology: Creator Music Technology
- Business Operations: Business Operations
- Community / Public Information: NoticeGrid
- Standalone Product: SONARA launchpad workspace

## Entity Colors

- Parent: cyan, teal, amber
- Creator: fuchsia, violet, cyan
- Business: green, aqua, neutral gray
- Community: blue, aqua, light neutral
- Launchpad: amber, cyan, slate

## Design Logic

Shared geometry improves recognition across web, mobile, signage, dashboards, and merchandise. Distinct category colors help users understand which company workspace they are using.

## Accessibility

- Use high contrast on dark backgrounds.
- Do not place pale text on pale surfaces.
- Preserve visible focus states.
- Do not recolor marks outside the token system.
- Test small app-icon sizes before shipping.

## Typography

Use clean, readable sans-serif typography. Prefer practical hierarchy over decorative lettering.

## Motion Guidance

The marks are motion-ready, but motion should be subtle: connection pulse, waveform rise, workflow cube rotation, civic beacon pulse, or launch chevron lift.

## Legal Warning

Working brand names, marks, and colors require trademark/domain review and qualified legal review before final public launch.
