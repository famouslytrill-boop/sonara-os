# Active Tools

SONARA Creator Business OS ships with five active tools. These are practical work surfaces that sit on top of A&R Intelligence, the Decision Engine, and the Revenue Pathway Engine.

## Prompt Vault

Stores and shapes reusable prompt systems for:

- songs
- release plans
- content angles
- visual direction
- local offer kits

## Artist OS

Organizes the artist system:

- identity
- catalog direction
- positioning
- saved projects
- release history

## Content Studio

Turns strategy into content planning:

- hook angles
- rollout notes
- caption direction
- short-form ideas
- campaign tasks

## Visual Builder

Frames visual execution:

- cover direction
- visual references
- brand consistency
- export asset needs

## Local Business Kits

Packages practical local creator business planning:

- studio offers
- service menus
- local release campaigns
- creator bundles
- operational checklists

Local Business Kits are planning tools only. They do not guarantee income, clients, bookings, placements, or market outcomes.
