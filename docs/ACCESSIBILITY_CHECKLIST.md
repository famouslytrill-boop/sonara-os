# Accessibility Checklist

Launch accessibility checks:

- Text contrast is readable on public and app backgrounds.
- Buttons have clear labels.
- Form labels describe the field purpose.
- Character limits are visible before export.
- Pages can be scanned on mobile.
- Help text explains setup-required and rights-blocked states.
- Do not rely on color alone for status.
- Keep touch targets large enough for mobile.
- Avoid dense jargon and unexplained acronyms.
