# Word Source Policy

Local SONARA OS™ word suggestions do not require attribution.

External sources require review:

- Store source title.
- Store source URL when available.
- Store license note.
- Store retrieval date.
- Do not copy definitions into commercial output as if original.

Urban Dictionary content is user-generated and may be copyrighted, offensive, inaccurate, or region-specific. Verify meaning and rights before using in commercial output.

Do not bulk scrape slang sources or bulk download external dictionaries into the app at launch.
