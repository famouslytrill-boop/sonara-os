# No Public Kit Marketplace Launch Policy

SONARA Exchange™ marketplace features are delayed for launch.

At launch, SONARA Vault™ is for:

- organizing assets
- classifying rights
- verifying usage status
- exporting user-owned or rights-cleared project materials
- preparing personal Vault Kit Exports

SONARA does not sell third-party sample packs at launch.

Marketplace features should remain delayed until these requirements are ready:

- rights verification
- seller compliance
- payout compliance
- moderation
- storage cost controls
- refund and support policy
- marketplace terms
