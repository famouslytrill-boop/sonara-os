# Design Intelligence Engine

SONARA design should feel warm, premium, music-tech, welcoming, mobile-first, conversion-focused, readable, and simple.

## Theme

- Background: `#08070B`
- Surface: `#121018`
- Card: `#191522`
- Soft Card: `#211B2D`
- Border: `#332A40`
- Primary Text: `#F9FAFB`
- Secondary Text: `#C4BFD0`
- Muted Text: `#8F879C`
- Primary Accent: `#9B5CFF`
- Warm Accent: `#FFB454`
- Cyan Accent: `#2DD4BF`
- Pink Accent: `#F472B6`

Avoid neon clutter, corporate gray prison, white cards in dark workspaces, fake luxury, casino-looking design, and overloaded dashboards.
