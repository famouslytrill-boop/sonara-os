# Brand Legal Review Warning

This repository contains working brand names, logo directions, and design tokens. They are not legal clearance.

Before production launch:

- Run trademark searches.
- Run domain and social handle searches.
- Review conflicts by class/category.
- Consult a qualified attorney.
- Confirm whether and how the trademark symbol should be used.
- Do not use the registered mark symbol unless registration exists.

Do not claim:

- Trademark clearance.
- Official government partnership.
- Guaranteed launch success.
- Guaranteed revenue or market outcome.
