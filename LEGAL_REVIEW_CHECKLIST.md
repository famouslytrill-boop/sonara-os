# Legal Review Checklist

Qualified legal review is required before paid public launch.

- Terms of Service
- Privacy Policy
- Refund Policy
- Cookie Policy
- Acceptable Use
- Accessibility
- Disclaimers
- Earnings disclaimer
- AI/tooling disclaimer
- Payment terms
- Data processing
- Security policy
- Commercial email/CAN-SPAM reminders
- Subprocessor notice
- Stripe restricted-business considerations
- Minor/child usage restrictions
