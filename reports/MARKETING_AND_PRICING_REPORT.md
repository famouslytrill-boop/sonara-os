# SONARA Marketing and Pricing Report

Date: 2026-07-15

## Customer-facing structure

- `/products`, `/service-catalog`, `/free-tools`, `/pricing`, and `/how-it-works` provide the shared discovery path.
- Business Builder, Creator Studio, and Growth Studio each have a distinct landing page, dashboard/workspace route family, tutorial, catalog, tools, and support path.
- Four canonical tutorials and product tutorial aliases are registered.
- The generated sitemap includes only intentionally public pages; protected and setup-only operations are excluded.

## Pricing and access

- The Free tier is displayed as `$0` and requires no checkout.
- Existing configured paid plan cards and Stripe price readiness are preserved.
- Free tools remain available to authenticated users without a paid entitlement.
- Paid records and tools remain protected by payment-backed entitlement checks.
- Missing Stripe configuration produces setup-required status instead of a fake checkout success.

## Claims boundary

The release does not claim verified conversion performance, market leadership, guaranteed outcomes, or production purchase completion. Pricing, taxes, refund language, subscription disclosures, trademarks, and all marketing/legal claims require owner and counsel review before promotion.
