# Legal Review

The legal pages included in the Express app are operational placeholders for owner review and launch readiness.

Pages requiring qualified legal review before paid public launch:

- `/legal/terms`
- `/legal/privacy`
- `/legal/refund-policy`
- `/legal/cookie-policy`
- `/legal/acceptable-use`
- `/legal/accessibility`

This repository does not provide legal advice. A qualified attorney should review the final public terms, privacy disclosures, refund commitments, cookie posture, acceptable use restrictions, and accessibility statements before paid launch.
