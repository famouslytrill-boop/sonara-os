# Product Strategy

SONARA Industries is affordable launch infrastructure for small service businesses, creators, and low-budget founders.

## Product companies

- Business Builder: offers, intake, customer records, booking/payment readiness, launch checklist.
- Creator Studio: asset catalog, creator offers, release/content checklist, monetization readiness, media/customer records.
- Growth Studio: campaign workspace, lead follow-up, consent-safe checklist, automation readiness, growth records.

Each module must produce deterministic useful output or return `setup_required` with instructions.
