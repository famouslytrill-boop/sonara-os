# Mathematical Models

Implemented in:
- `apps/api/app/formulas.py`
- `apps/web/lib/formulas.ts`

Models:
- geometric weighted readiness score with epsilon protection
- labor percentage
- recipe cost
- menu target price
- alert severity
- security risk
- queue utilization
- exponential smoothing
- moat score
- pricing score

These are decision-support models. They do not guarantee revenue, compliance, safety, or operational outcomes.
